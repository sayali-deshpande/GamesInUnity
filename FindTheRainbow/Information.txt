This game is created for the Citrus Jam #2, which had a duration of 48 hours. Theme: "Color is everything".
Move: W/A/S/D or Arrow keys and Space to Jump, hold down Shift to walk slowly.

Your mission is to collect spheres in order to form a rainbow. The spheres will get spawned randomly, 
you'll have to collect all the seven colored spheres.
The spheres have a minimum active period of 30 seconds and maximum 70 seconds. You cannot collect same colored sphere twice. 
Your collected spheres would be displayed at the top of your screen.

Play game at: https://connect.unity.com/mg/other/findtherainbow
